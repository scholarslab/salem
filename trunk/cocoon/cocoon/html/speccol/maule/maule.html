Truth Held Forth
and
Maintained
According to the Testimony of the holy
Prophets, Christ and his Apostles recorded in the
holy Scriptures.

With some Account of the Judgments of the Lord lately
inflicted upon New - England by Witch-craft.

To which is added, Something concerning the Fall of Adam,
his state in the Fall, and way of Restoration to God
again, with many other weighty things, necessary for
People to weigh and consider.

Written in true Love to the Souls of my Neighbours, and
all Men, which includeth that Love to them, as to my self,
by
Thomas Maule
Printed in the Year 1695.
