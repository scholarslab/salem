Map of Salem Village in 1692
By W.P. Upham, 1866

From Charles W. Upham's Salem Witchcraft, With an Account of Salem Village and a History of Opinions on Witchcraft and Kindred Spirits (1867).

This map shows the locations of major landmarks, farms, land grants, physical features, and the dwellings of promient and important residents in Salem during 1692.
